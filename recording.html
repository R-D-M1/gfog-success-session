Common questions

These come up on almost every session. If yours isn't here, just message us.

Why do my rankings change from day to day?
Google's algorithm is constantly updating. Your rankings can shift based on competitor activity, search patterns, time of day, and algorithm tweaks. Small fluctuations are completely normal. What matters is the overall trend over weeks and months, not individual days.
How long until I see results?
Most clients start seeing movement within 4 to 6 weeks, but results look different for everyone. For some people, results mean going from not showing up at all to being found in their local town. For others, it's the review replies saving them hours every week, especially if you're a restaurant or a business with a lot of reviews coming in. Some clients tell us the biggest value is just having the grid scans so they can actually see where they rank across their area. The point is, the service does different things for different businesses. The first month is always groundwork. By month two you'll see your rankings improving. Month three is where the compound effect kicks in and things really start to shift. We'd encourage you to think about what results look like for your specific situation.
Can I change my target keyword?
Yes. If your business focus has shifted or you want to target a different service, just let us know. We'll update your profile categories, description, and content strategy to match. Bear in mind it can take a few weeks for Google to re-evaluate your profile for new keywords.
Do reviews really matter that much?
Yes. Reviews are one of the strongest ranking signals Google looks at. They also convert browsers into buyers. 93% of people read reviews before choosing a local business. Consistency matters more than volume. Even 1 or 2 a month puts you ahead of most competitors.
What if a competitor outranks me?
It depends on why. Proximity plays the biggest role. If they're physically closer to where someone is searching, they'll naturally rank higher in that spot. For everything else (prominence, relevance), that's what we're working on for you every week. If you notice a specific competitor consistently outranking you, let us know and we can take a closer look.
How do I respond to a bad review?
As part of the service, we reply to all of your positive reviews for you. For negative reviews, we'd recommend you reply to those yourself because you know the situation and the customer. The key is to stay professional, acknowledge the concern, and offer to resolve it offline. Never argue publicly. A thoughtful reply to a negative review actually builds trust with potential customers who read it.

Before and afters, jobs in progress, your van, your team, anything real performs way better than stock images. We geotag every photo to boost your local rankings. You'll find the link in the resources section below.
When do I get my tap card?
We send your Google Review Tap Card out as part of your onboarding. If you haven't received yours yet, just drop us a message and we'll get one sorted for you straight away. It's included free with your service.
What are Google updates?
Every week we publish an update to your Google Business Profile. These are short pieces of content with images and keyword-rich copy that signal to Google your business is active. They appear on your profile and last about seven days, which is why we keep them rolling. These are profile updates, not social media posts.
