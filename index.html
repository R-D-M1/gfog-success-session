01 Behind the Scenes

What we actually do for you every week

You might have watched Ryan's video before signing up. Now I want to show you in more detail what's happening behind the scenes on your profile every single week.

1

Profile Optimisation

We make sure Google knows exactly what you do and where you do it. Categories, services, business description, attributes. We're telling Google what you want to rank for.

2

Weekly Google Updates

Every week we publish an update to your profile using real images and keyword-rich copy. These updates signal to Google that your business is active. They last about seven days, so we keep them rolling.

3

Geotagged Photo Uploads

Every photo taken on a phone has location data embedded in it. Before we upload your photos, we adjust the geotag to target the areas where you need to improve. This gives Google a stronger location signal for your business.

4

Profile Protection & Lock

You'd be surprised what people try. Competitors changing your phone number to theirs. Random users editing your business hours to say you're closed. People swapping your categories so you stop ranking for your main service. Google lets anyone suggest edits, and if you're not watching, they go through automatically. We lock your profile down, monitor for unauthorised changes, and reject anything that shouldn't be there. It happens more than you'd think.

5

Spam Listing Removal

Fake or spammy competitor listings can push your ranking down. We actively report and remove these so you're competing on a level playing field.

03 How Rankings Work

How Google decides who shows up

When someone searches "plumber near me" or "window cleaner Tamworth", Google uses three things to decide which businesses appear in the map pack.

📍
ProximityHow close you are to the person searching. This is the biggest factor and the one you can't control. It's why rankings change depending on where someone is standing.
⭐
ProminenceHow well-known and trusted your business is online. Reviews, citations, website links, and profile activity all play into this. This is the one we can influence the most.
🎯
RelevanceHow well your profile matches the search. If someone searches "emergency plumber" but your profile only says "plumber", you're less relevant. We make sure you're matched up properly.

How to read your ranking update

You'll get a ranking update straight to your inbox. Here's what the key numbers mean so you know exactly what you're looking at.

inbox — your ranking update
Average Rank
4.2

Your average position across all grid points. Lower is better. Under 5 is strong. Under 3 means you're dominating your area.

Top 3 Visibility
68%

The percentage of grid points where you're in the map pack (top 3). This is the number to watch. 80% of people only look at the top 3 results.

"Plumber near me"

The keyword we're tracking. This matches what your ideal customers are actually typing into Google when they need your service.

Trend Direction
↑ +3

How your rankings are moving week on week. Don't worry about small dips. What matters is the overall trend over weeks and months.

📊

Your full scan history

Every update includes a link to your full scan history. You can see all your past scans, track how your rankings have changed over time, and even check where your competitors are ranking. It's all in there.

💡 Don't panic about fluctuations

Rankings naturally move around day to day. Google is constantly testing. What matters is the trend over 4–8 weeks, not any single report. If you've been with us less than 90 days, your profile is still building momentum and that's completely normal.
